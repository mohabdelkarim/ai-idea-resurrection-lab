This could utilize existing patterns like `container probes` and `Pod lifecycle hooks`. Classes/APIs such as `Batch`, `Job`, and `Pod` controllers would need to be updated to handle this logic. The solution might also involve enhancing `kubectl` to provide easier management of such Jobs.
