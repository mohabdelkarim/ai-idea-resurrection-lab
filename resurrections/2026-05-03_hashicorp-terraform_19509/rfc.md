The motivation behind this proposal is to provide a more robust and flexible way to execute external commands and capture their output within Terraform. This feature will enable users to automate complex workflows and integrate Terraform with other tools and systems.

The proposed provider will expose a `command` attribute that accepts an inline command, and an `output` attribute that returns the captured output. The provider will also handle errors and exceptions properly, and provide a clear and concise error message to the user.

## Drawbacks
One potential drawback of this proposal is the potential security risk associated with executing external commands. To mitigate this risk, the provider will only allow the execution of commands that are explicitly whitelisted by the user.

An alternative approach would be to use an existing Terraform provider, such as the `null` provider, to execute external commands and capture their output. However, this approach would require significant modifications to the existing provider, and may not provide the same level of flexibility and customization as the proposed provider.
